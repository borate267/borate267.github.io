    • Abstract: Ensuring fairness while allocating radio resources to users is crucial in modern large-scale communication systems. This paper proposes scalable parallel partitioning-based Proportional Fair (PF) scheduling schemes for multi-carrier transmission systems and empirically demonstrates its scalability for large number of users and sub-carriers, while maintaining fairness.
